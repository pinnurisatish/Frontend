Manohar Kesireddy
President & CEO
At Paññã, Manohar is responsible for day-to-day operations as well as leading the company's product development and technology strategy. He has over 15 years of experience in corporate at various roles and holds several patents in technology. He has successfully led development teams at Verizon Telecom. Manohar holds a bachelor's degree from IIT Madras, India and a Master's degree in computer science from University of Texas at Dallas. He is also a member of board for the non-profit organization India Dreams.
In his spare time, Manohar practices Vipassana meditation and is working hard to make par scores in golf!
Prakash Nallagatla
Co-Founder/Vice-President
Prakash Nallagatla Co-Founder/Vice-President Prakash heads the development effort at Paññã and is responsible for product planning and development, engineering, deployment and service assurance for Paññã products. In his spare time, Prakash avidly follows professional leagues of basketball, football and enjoys playing a round of golf. He is also very passionate about electronics and gadgets that impact human life as we know today.
